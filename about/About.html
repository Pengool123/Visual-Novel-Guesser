How To Play / Meanings

You have 8 guesses to guess the visual novel.

You start with 5 tags. All non-spoiler content tags with a strength of 1.5 or higher are accepted.

If the visual novel you put in has any similar tags, they will be put in.

Hint 2 will give you the languages (official or not). Anything outside JP, EN, KO, RU, CN, ES and VI will be marked as 'âž•'.

Hint 3 is a ranking range (inclusive) of where it lies

Hint 4 will give another range, this time for the amount of votes.

Hint 5 will tell you if an 18+ version/patch exists or not.

Hint 6 will give the VNDB's length of the visual novel.

Hint 7 will display the top quote, if any.

The final hint will be a blured image of the cover art.
